Purpose of the discovery

This discovery aims to understand the users and problem for online careers journeys. And how might we provide users with a starting point for impartial information about careers, jobs, education and training.

The user groups are young people, adults in work but wanting to change, unemployed people looking for work, careers advisors and DWP work coaches, parents, and teachers.
